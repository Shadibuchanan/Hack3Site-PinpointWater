Overcoming The Water Crisis Together

Over the next few years, the demand for clean water will continue increase as the population grows. With so much water being lost to pollution and poor irrigation infrastructure, the future looks bleak, but together we can create a better tomorrow for everyone. Please consider donating to people in need of water or donating to organizations that are working to ensure that we will have clean water in the future.

How to Help

If you are looking to help solve the water crisis, please consider donating to any of the organizations on the right. You can find organizations focused on global change in the default organizations list, or you can find organizations focused on providing water to certain areas by selecting one of the location pins on the map.

Organizations

Generosity.org

Generosity.org is a clean water organization that is focused on providing clean, sustainable drinking water to developing countries around the world. So far, they have completed over 800 wells in 20 different countries.

Thirst Project

Thirst Project is a water organization that educates students about the clean water crisis and mobilizes them to take action. In addition to raising awareness, Thirst Project has boots in India, Uganda, Ethiopia, El Salvador, Kenya, and Colombia. They focus primarily on building hand pump wells that typically last over 40 years.

WaterAid

WaterAid is a water organization that transforms lives by providing clean water, hygiene and sanitation hardware in the world’s most vulnerable and remote communities. Clean water that’s close to home makes the chore of collecting water a matter of minutes, not hours. This frees up precious time so girls can go to school and people can work and start businesses. Clean water really is just the beginning, it unlocks potential and empowers people to thrive.
